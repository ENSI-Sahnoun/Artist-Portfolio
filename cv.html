Sculptor & Artist

Tunisian sculptor and visual artist whose practice spans sculpture, calligraphy, and multidisciplinary exhibitions across Tunisia and internationally.

Biography

  • Born on September 18, 1971, in Sfax.
  • Obtained the baccalaureate in June 1991.
  • Studied at the Institut Technologique d'Art, d'Architecture et d'Urbanisme de Tunis (ITAAUT).
  • Earned a degree in Fine Arts, specializing in sculpture.
  • Taught in secondary schools from 1995 to 2000.
  • Joined the Higher Institute of Arts and Crafts in Sfax (ISAMS) in 2000.
  • Produced sculptures for the opening and closing of the Mediterranean Games Tunisia 2001.
  • Member of art-related associations in Sfax.
  • Participated in national and international exhibitions, festivals, and symposiums in Tunisia, France, Germany, Algeria, Morocco, Libya, UAE, Oman, Iraq, Iran, Jordan, and India.
