about
Born on September 18, 1971, in Sfax, he obtained his baccalaureate in June 1991. He pursued his studies at the Institut Technologique d'Art, d'Architecture et d'Urbanisme de Tunis (ITAAUT), where he earned a degree in Fine Arts with a specialization in sculpture. From 1995 to 2000, he taught in secondary schools before joining the Higher Institute of Arts and Crafts in Sfax in 2000 (ISAMS).
